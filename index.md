# ReciDecks

Simple, local-first recipe management.

---

## Privacy Policy

*Last updated: 30 April 2026*

### Overview

ReciDecks is designed to be **local-first and privacy-friendly**.

Your data stays on your device and is not transmitted to any external servers. We do not collect personal information in the current version of the app.

---

### Data Storage

All data you add is stored locally on your device:

- Recipes  
- Ingredient lists  
- Notes  
- Recipe collections ("decks")  
- Imported images  

---

### Permissions

ReciDecks may request access to the following device features:

- **Notifications** — used to send local alerts for cooking timers  
- **Camera** — used to scan and import recipes from photos  
- **Photo Library** — used to import and save recipe images  

All data related to these features is processed on-device and is not collected or transmitted.

---

### Data Collection

ReciDecks does not collect, store, or share personal data in the current version of the app.

---

### Future Features

We may introduce optional features such as:

- Cloud sync  
- Accounts  
- Recipe sharing  
- Personalised recommendations  

If these are added, this policy will be updated.

---

### Your Control

You are always in control of your data:

- Edit or delete recipes at any time  
- Remove all data by uninstalling the app
