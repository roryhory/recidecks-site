# Version 1.0.0

*Released: 28 April 2026*

---

## What's New

Welcome to ReciDecks - your personal recipe card collection!

This is the very first release. Here's what's included:

---

### Save Recipes from Any URL

Found a recipe you love online? Just paste the URL and ReciDecks will automatically extract the title, ingredients, steps and image - instantly turning it into a clean recipe card.

**Get straight to your favourite recipes**
No more lost tabs. No more screenshots. No more cluttered recipe pages.

ReciDecks is free to use with up to 8 saved recipes. Unlock **unlimited recipes** with a one-time purchase of £2.99 / $2.99 - no subscription.

---

### Recipe Cards

Turn your favourite recipes into personalisable recipe cards. Use a recipe URL, photo of a cookbook, or enter a recipe manually.

- **Edit Mode** - long press anywhere on a recipe card to edit it. Remove ingredients, adjust timings, add a personalised photo, and more
- **Shopping List** - build a shopping list straight from your ingredients
- **Servings scaler** - adjust the recipe serving number to rescale the ingredients
- **Cook Mode** - keep your screen awake while you cook and highlight recipe steps
- **Timers** - start a timer by tapping a timing instruction
- **Favourites** - add a recipe to your favourites to ensure it is placed at the top of your collections
- **Sharing** - share cards with other ReciDecks users or share as an image

---


### Decks

Organise your recipes into themed collections for any occasion.

- **Customise** - add a personal touch to your collections by changing the deck colour - long press in an open deck and tap the palette icon to get started
- **Sort-by** - organise your entire recipe collection by deck
- **Share** - share your collections with other ReciDecks users

---

### Home

Your cooking hub. Quick access to what you use most.

- **Pins** - pin your most used recipes and decks straight to your home page
- **Shopping List** - keep track of ingredients for all recipes on your list
